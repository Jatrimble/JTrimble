Welcome to Jelani Trimble's Design Practice

At the heart of design is the relationship between people and the spaces they inhabit. My practice explores the intricate connections between scale, geography, culture, and emotion, striving to create environments that not only serve functional needs but also foster well-being. I focus on how aesthetics, space, and light shape our mental and physical health, with a commitment to sustainable and human-centered design.
